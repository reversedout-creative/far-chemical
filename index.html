About Far Chemical

FAR Chemical is a leading U.S.-based custom, contract and toll manufacturer of specialty organic chemicals. We have specific expertise in the handling and production of complex and hazardous chemistries. These include bromine, fluorine, and chlorine-based materials, as well as compounds that are sensitive to moisture or air.
